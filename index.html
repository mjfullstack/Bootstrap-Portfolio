Michael Johnson is a highly adaptable ASIC/FPGA design, design verification, and field failure analysis engineer. He has several years of experience in Application Specific Integrated Circuit and Field Programmable Gate Array design, with additional experience circuit in card assembly design.

His experience includes Verilog RTL design and verification of multiple Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) cryptographic engines. He engineered Differential Power Analysis (DPA) prevention techniques for Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) hardware module, resulting in Dynamic Data Masking patent with Comcast.

He is currently studying full-stack web development at UCSD Extension. His experience including career changes have enabled him to quickly learn and contribute to new projects in various hardware and software roles. Currently, he is studying blockchain technology while completing the bootcamp program.
